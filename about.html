Our Story

Catarino did not set out to become an electrician but he did dream of having his own business one day. Catarino began working as a service technician with his brother in-law in the early 1990’s gaining hands on experience. Electrical work came naturally to him. He was detailed oriented, good in math, and liked to solve problems. After five years of learning the trade, he set out to work on small projects on his own racking referrals. After years of hard work he established a growing and loyal clientele that included home and business owners and eventually general contractors.

Today, Catarino still takes pride in providing quality service knowing how important home electrical service and safety are to home and business owners. He has instilled these values to his son Uriel. Together they continue to grow this family-own business.

Uriel is a second-generation electrician. He started accompanying his dad to work as a young boy. Uriel was exposed to the basics of electrical work during many weekends and summers of working with his father. He continued to build on this field knowledge working with his father throughout high school, college and graduate school. After working on the field of education for several years Uriel decided to formalized his training as an electrician and join his father, Catarino, in the family business.

In 2013, Uriel obtained his electrical license. He holds a City of Chicago Supervising Electrician’s License and a City of Cicero Electrical License. Additionally, he works to stay current about the field and code through training in this field through workshops, seminars, and conferences to stay up to date.

As the supervising electrician at Montoya LLC, he is responsible for the administration and management. Under his management the business has grown to 2 full time service technicians and expanded to larger projects.
