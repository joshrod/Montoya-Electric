Our Commitment

Our Ideals

Safety

Montoya Electric LLC upholds safety in two critical ways. First, we work to ensure safety in all of our projects – big or small. Second, the services we provide are safe as they are performed by a trained and experienced professional protecting your home, business, or investment.

Integrity

Montoya LLC. will provide a detailed overview of recommended services so that you can make an informed decision before any work starts. We place the client’s needs first and keep you informed every step of the way. We behave with integrity and strive to be transparent with work scope, timeline, and cost in from the initiation of the project to the end.

Efficiency

We will arrive when scheduled. No more waiting all day for contractors. We work to ensure that your project is completed in the established timeline.

Quality

We have a high standard and guarantee the client’s satisfaction. We pride ourselves on our reputation that includes quality on-time service, accurate estimates, and a personal relationship with the customers.

Professionalism

We strive for a positive customer experience from your estimate to the time a job is completed by making communication a top priority. We will explain the repair thoroughly and will not begin work before you approval.
